Runescape & Life






It all started back then in grade 5, i met this guy and day by day we became good friends, and as the days passed he introduced me to this game called "RuneScape" i Tried it out and got Very addicted to it, Years passed and i kept still playing it. I knew everything about this game, i became a professional fighter and played in tournaments that were hosted by Jagex, Owner of Runescape LTD.

However in some point of my life i Realised that im truly wasting my time and energy over a game that is not worth the hustle. So i quit the game, Now i only login to the game only once a while to see whats going but im probably never returning back.

It was a beatiful game that captured my mind and soul, But we have to go along with life to survive in this hard cruel world.
